GO TO GAMES

how to play?

Prepare phase

Before the game begins, the players are briefed about their mission and divided into teams, each with distinct roles. They receive an encrypted message from their superior, setting the stage for their mission.

1. Mission Briefing:

Players are given a dossier containing the background of their mission, detailing the threat they are facing and the objectives to complete. A detailed map of London, highlighting key locations of interest, is distributed among the teams.

2. Role Assignment:

Each player is assigned a specific role within the spy team. Possible roles include: The Analyst – Expert in decoding messages and solving puzzles. The Operative – Skilled in navigating through dangerous situations and collecting vital information. Tcodes, and piece together information.

Play phase

The mission is underway. Players must work together to uncover hidden secrets, solve puzzles, and gather intelligence, all while racing against the clock. This phase is dynamic and interactive, requiring constant teamwork, critical thinking, and quick decision-making.

1. First Clue Unveiled:

The game begins with the team receiving their first clue, which will lead them to a specific location or task. Players must use their roles effectively: the Analyst decodes messages, the Operative navigates through challenges, the Tech Specialist handles the gadgets, and the Leader coordinates efforts.

2. Decrypting Codes and Solving Puzzles:

Players will encounter multiple encrypted messages and puzzles that must be solved using clues hidden around the room or the game space. The Encrypted Envelope holds key information that will lead to new tasks, while Invisible Ink messages provide critical hints when viewed under the special light. Files containing sensitive information will be unlocked when players discover the correct passwords or passcodes.
